The Wonderful World of Birds

Exploring Nature's Aerial Marvels

Birds are among the most captivating creatures on our planet. With over 10,000 species worldwide, they display an incredible diversity of colors, behaviors, and adaptations that have fascinated humans for centuries.

Did You Know? The smallest bird in the world is the Bee Hummingbird, measuring just 2 inches long, while the largest is the Ostrich, standing up to 9 feet tall!

Bird Conservation

Create Habitat

Plant native trees and shrubs to provide food and shelter for local bird species.

Prevent Window Strikes

Apply decals to windows to help birds see glass and avoid collisions.

Keep Cats Indoors

Domestic cats are a major threat to bird populations worldwide.

Avoid Pesticides

Chemicals can harm birds directly or reduce their insect food sources.
